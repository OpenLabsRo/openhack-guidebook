# Summary

We know this is a long document. But if you read nothing else, read this page. Here’s a one-pager checklist to make your OpenHack 2025 experience the best it can be.

### **Before the event**

- Check your email and follow us on Instagram **@openhackro** for all updates. Also join the **Whatsapp Group**.
- Pack your **valid ID** and **registration confirmation email**
- Don’t forget your **medication (if you have a medical condition)**, **laptop, charger, headphones, and power bank**
- If you don’t have a team: we’ll help match solos before the hackathon starts — but coming with friends is best for team chemistry
- Think ahead: the **theme will be announced 48h before** so you can brainstorm ideas

### **During the event**

- **Check-in:** Nov 8, 10:00–16:00 at the Central Library entrance (bring ID + confirmation email)
- Doors open at 10:00 AM → find your table & set up; Opening Ceremony starts at **11:30 PM**
- You’ll receive your **badge**, required for entry at all times
- Food, snacks, and coffee are free during the whole event
- Key moments: **Pizza Night (Nov 8 evening), Sunrise Ritual (Nov 9 morning), Final Judging & Awards (Nov 9 afternoon)**
- Submission deadline: Nov 9, 10:00 AM
- Judges pick the top 3 → public votes the final winner
