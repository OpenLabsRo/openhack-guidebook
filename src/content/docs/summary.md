# Summary

We know this is a long document. But if you read nothing else, read this page. Here’s a one-pager checklist to make your OpenHack 2025 experience the best it can be.

### **Before the event**

- Make sure you are part of the **Whatsapp** group and follow **@openhackro.**
- Pack your **valid ID** and **registration confirmation email**
- Don’t forget your **medication (if you have a medical condition)**, **laptop, charger, headphones, and power bank**
- Think ahead: the **theme will be announced 48h before** so you can brainstorm ideas. **No coding allowed** until the **start** of the hackathon.

### **During the event**

- **Check-in:** Nov 8, 10:00–15:00 at the **UPB Central Library** entrance (bring ID + confirmation email)
- Doors open at 10:00 AM → find your table & set up; Opening Ceremony starts at **12:00 PM**
- You’ll receive your **badge and wristband**, required for entry at all times
- Food, snacks, and coffee are free during the whole event
- Key moments: **Pizza Night (Nov 8 evening), Sunrise Ritual (Nov 9 morning), Final Judging & Awards (Nov 9 afternoon)**
- GitHub Link and Project Description deadline Nov 8, 18:00
- Judges pick the top 3 → public votes the final winner
